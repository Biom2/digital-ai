02 · What we heard

Five consistent themes
emerged from the interviews

1

Manual reporting burden is universal

Across BESS Delivery, Finance, and Asset Management, a lot of time goes on manual data compilation, monthly reports, and status updates. The asks for automation were consistent.

2

MasdarMind maturity gap

The Databricks/Azure platform exists but most functions aren't connected to it yet. The infrastructure investment is there — the gap is in ingestion, governance, and making it accessible to teams.

3

BI governance window is closing

Multiple Power BI dashboards exist in isolation with no single source of truth. Without a governance standard now, the fragmentation will compound as the portfolio grows.

4

Undigitised workflows across functions

Legal approvals (DOA/CRS), HSSE reporting, cash requests, and contract reviews are still largely manual or on email. Low complexity, high frequency — ideal automation candidates.

5

Business-facing tool gap

Non-technical teams currently have no direct access to the data or AI tools being built. Dashboards and simple AI interfaces are the bridge — and without them, adoption stalls regardless of what's built in the background.

07 · The foundation

MasdarMind — the central platform
powering every use case

All 32 use cases in this roadmap either feed into or are powered by MasdarMind — Masdar UK's data lakehouse on Databricks and Azure. The platform is already partially in use. The 2026 roadmap is about extending it systematically across the business.
Bronze Layer
Raw Ingestion
SCADA, Xero, TIPALTI, SharePoint, Arenko, Accure feeds land here. No transformation, full history preserved.
Silver Layer
Cleaned & Governed
Standardised schemas, data quality checks, Unity Catalog governance. One version of the truth per domain.
Gold Layer
Business-Ready
KPIs, aggregates, and ML features consumed by Power BI dashboards, AI agents, and the MasdarMind App Portal.
Already live
HR Policy Bot on SharePoint · Pouyan's anomaly detection in production · Internal Audit Dashboard (in test) · KYC workflow automation for Legal (in test) · Xero PoC underway with Tim

Databricks Apps UAE data centre config · Xero paid API plan · Data Engineer contractor onboarding
