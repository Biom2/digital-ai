01 · What we heard

Five signals emerged independently,
across every function

No prompting. No leading questions. The same pain points surfaced in conversation after conversation — from Asset Management to Legal, from Finance to BESS Delivery. That convergence is the strongest possible signal that these are systemic issues, not individual complaints.

1

Manual reporting is a universal pain point

Every single team assembles monthly reports by hand. No shared data layer, no automation, no single source of truth. Asset Management, BESS Delivery, Internal Audit, Finance — the same root cause everywhere.

Stephen (VP BESS Delivery): his team loses one full Monday per person per month assembling reports.
2

MasdarMind is more mature than most teams realise

Five people from five different functions independently named MasdarMind as the right platform — without coordination. Pouyan has anomaly detection in production. Tim has built the Medallion architecture. The risk is not starting from zero. The risk is that these assets stay invisible and ungoverned.

Lars (Commercial): endorsed MasdarMind as a "global lighthouse project".
3

BI governance window is closing

Simon is building Power BI dashboards independently. Pouyan wants Databricks Apps. Without a standard set now, every team will have its own disconnected tools within 6 months. Sahil flagged this risk explicitly before it was even mentioned.

4

Core workflows run on email and Excel, not systems

Approval chains, contract routing, employee onboarding, payment authorisations — none are digitised. Every workflow relies on someone manually identifying the right approvers and tracking progress in a spreadsheet. As the portfolio grows, operational risk grows proportionally.

Simon (FP&A): a £20M cash request takes half a day of manual processing.
5

Business-facing digital tools are the untapped opportunity

A significant gap exists between the digital tools Masdar UK has today and what the business needs: a project management platform for BESS construction, asset performance tools for offshore wind, predictive maintenance with JV partners. The gap is real, identified, and addressable.

Lars: BESS dispatch optimisation is "the biggest AI lever" — indicative ROI: 2.7–10.9× over 5 years.

▶ C · MasdarMind — Data Platform Architecture

MasdarMind is the UK's Databricks/Azure data platform, already operational with:

Architecture: Azure ADLS Gen2, Databricks with Unity Catalog, Medallion architecture (Bronze / Silver / Gold). Automated SCADA pipelines for 3 offshore wind assets. Power BI and Grafana dashboards.

In production: BESS anomaly detection AI model with embedded LLM (Gemma) — no data leaves the secure workspace. SCADA ingestion for London Array, Dudgeon, and Race Bank.

In progress: Xero API integration (PoC working for 1 entity, paid plan needed for 30+). HR policy AI agent on Copilot Studio (live), migration to MasdarMind RAG pipeline planned. Baltic Eagle SCADA connection.

Key blocker: Databricks Apps blocked because data centre is hosted in UAE — configuration issue, not a technical rebuild, but requires HQ IT escalation.
