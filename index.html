01 Alignment 03 / 08
01Alignment

Executing the Group's AI ambition on the ground.

Group vision

AI that moves operational and commercial numbers.

The Group direction is explicit. AI earns its place by improving availability, lowering cost, accelerating execution, and lifting enterprise productivity. Technology is a means, not a milestone.

UK mandate

Translate that into Offshore Wind and BESS.

Masdar UK operationalises the direction for Offshore Wind and BESS.

Delivery model

Platform, portfolio, governance.

One data & AI platform (MasdarMind). A prioritised use case portfolio backed by named business owners. Governance built in from day one, aligned with the Group data management office.

Aligned with Group direction. Local operating model in place to deliver against it.
